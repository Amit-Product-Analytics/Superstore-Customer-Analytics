# Customer Retention & Churn Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on customer retention, churn analysis, and RFM segmentation in an e-commerce business.

The objective of this project is to understand customer loyalty, identify churn risks, analyze customer value, and improve retention strategies through data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

Customer retention is one of the most important growth drivers for any business.

This project answers the following business questions:

* What is the customer retention rate?
* What is the customer churn rate?
* Which customers are at risk of churning?
* Who are the most valuable customers?
* How much revenue is generated by VIP customers?
* How can retention be improved?

## Key Insights

* Customer retention remained strong across the business.
* VIP customers generated a significant share of total revenue.
* Repeat purchases played a major role in business growth.
* High-risk customers require targeted retention campaigns.
* RFM segmentation helped identify valuable and at-risk customer groups.
